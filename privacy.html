Privacy Policy for Status

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Introduction

Status ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the Status mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, display name, and phone number
  • Profile Information: Profile picture (optional), avatar color
  • Status Updates: Your availability status and custom messages (max 15 characters)
  • Friend Connections: Friend requests and accepted friendships
  • Music Activity: Currently playing music (track name, artist name) when you choose to share it

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and session duration
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • CloudKit Data: Data synchronized through Apple's CloudKit service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Status account
  • Display your availability status to your friends
  • Enable friend connections and social features
  • Show your music activity to friends when you're listening to music
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send notifications about friend requests and status updates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

Data Storage and Security

Apple CloudKit

Your data is stored using Apple's CloudKit service, which provides:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure cloud storage infrastructure
  • Data residency in Apple's data centers

Local Storage

Some data is cached locally on your device for:

  • Offline functionality
  • Widget display
  • Improved app performance

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication through iCloud
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and permissions

Data Sharing

Information Shared with Friends

When you add someone as a friend, they can see:

  • Your username and display name
  • Your profile picture (if set)
  • Your current availability status
  • Your custom status message (if set)
  • Your music activity (if you're listening to music)

Third-Party Services

We may share limited data with:

  • Apple CloudKit: For data storage and synchronization
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For sending notifications
  • Apple Music API: For displaying your music activity (only metadata, not listening history)

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable music sharing or other optional features

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Delete Account: Go to Settings → Delete Account
  • Update Profile: Go to Settings → Edit Profile
  • Disable Music Sharing: Go to Settings → Privacy → Disable Music Activity

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Status Updates: Expired statuses are automatically deleted after expiration
  • Friend Requests: Rejected requests are deleted after 90 days

Children's Privacy

Status is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your country of residence. By using Status, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy in the app
  • Sending a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of Status after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - for California users
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
