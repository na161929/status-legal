Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add friends?

To add a friend:

  1. Tap the "Add Friend" button on the main screen
  2. Search for your friend's username
  3. Send a friend request
  4. Once they accept, you'll see each other's statuses

How do I change my status?

Tap your current status at the top of the screen to open the status selector. Choose from 15 different status options like Free, Busy, In Class, Gaming, and more. You can also add a custom message (up to 15 characters).

Why can't my friends see my music activity?

To share music activity:

  • Make sure you have Apple Music installed
  • Ensure music sharing is enabled in Settings → Privacy
  • Music must be actively playing
  • Check that your friends have accepted your friend request

How do I add the widget to my home screen?

To add the Status widget:

  1. Long-press on your home screen
  2. Tap the "+" button in the top-left
  3. Search for "Status" and select it
  4. Choose your preferred widget size
  5. Tap "Add Widget"

How do I block someone?

To block a user:

  1. Go to their profile
  2. Tap the three dots (⋯) in the top-right
  3. Select "Block User"
  4. Confirm the action

Blocked users cannot see your status and cannot send you friend requests.

How do I report inappropriate content?

To report a user:

  1. Go to their profile
  2. Tap the three dots (⋯) in the top-right
  3. Select "Report User"
  4. Choose a reason and provide details
  5. Submit the report

All reports are reviewed by our team.

How do I delete my account?

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  2. Scroll down to "Delete Account"
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted within 30 days.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

If you're not receiving notifications:

  • Check that notifications are enabled in iPhone Settings → Status
  • Ensure Do Not Disturb is turned off
  • Try restarting the app
  • Check your internet connection

Is my data secure?

Yes! Status uses Apple's CloudKit for secure data storage with end-to-end encryption. Your status is only visible to friends you've accepted. We never sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Does Status work offline?

Status requires an internet connection to sync status updates. However, you can view cached friend statuses when offline. The widget shows the last-synced data when offline.

Can I use Status on multiple devices?

Yes! Status syncs across all your devices using iCloud. Just sign in with the same Apple ID on each device.

Troubleshooting

App is crashing

Try these steps:

  1. Force quit the app and restart it
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure you're running iOS 17.0 or later
  5. If the issue persists, contact support

Status updates not syncing

If status updates aren't syncing:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure iCloud is enabled for Status in Settings
  • Check iCloud storage isn't full
  • Try logging out and back in

Phone verification not working

If you're having trouble with phone verification:

  • Ensure you entered your phone number correctly with country code
  • Check that you have cellular service
  • Wait a few minutes and request a new code
  • Check your spam folder for the verification message
